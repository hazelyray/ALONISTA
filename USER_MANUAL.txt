1. GETTING STARTED - LOGIN
---------------------------

When you first open the system, you will see the login screen.

To log in:
  - Enter your username in the "Username" field
  - Enter your password in the "Password" field
  - Click the "Login" button

Default Admin Account:
  - Username: admin
  - Password: admin123

IMPORTANT: Change your password after first login for security!

After successful login, you will be taken to the Admin Dashboard.


2. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
---------------------

The Dashboard is the main screen you see after logging in. It shows:
  - Your name and role at the top
  - A menu on the left side with all available features
  - The main content area in the center

The Dashboard displays important statistics:
  - Total number of students
  - Number of enrolled students
  - Number of pending students
  - Number of teachers
  - Current school year information

You can always return to the Dashboard by clicking the "Dashboard" button in the 
left menu.


3. STUDENT MANAGEMENT
---------------------

Student Management allows you to add new students, view existing students, and 
re-enroll students for the new school year.

3.1 ADD STUDENT
---------------

To add a new student:
  1. Click "Student Management" in the left menu
  2. Click "Add Student" from the submenu
  3. Fill in the student information form:

     Personal Information:
     - Student ID Number (required)
     - First Name (required)
     - Middle Name (optional)
     - Last Name (required)
     - Date of Birth (required)
     - Gender (required)
     - Address (required)
     - Contact Number (required)
     - Email Address (optional)
     - Guardian Name (required)
     - Guardian Contact (required)

     Academic Information:
     - Grade Level (select 11 or 12)
     - Strand (select from available strands)
     - Section (select from available sections)
     - Enrollment Status (select Enrolled or Pending)
     - School Year & Semester (select from dropdown)

  4. Click "Save" to add the student
  5. A success message will appear if the student is added successfully

IMPORTANT: Make sure all required fields are filled before saving.

3.2 VIEW STUDENTS
-----------------

To view all students:
  1. Click "Student Management" in the left menu
  2. Click "View Students" from the submenu
  3. You will see a list of all students with their information
  4. You can search for students by:
     - Student ID
     - Name
     - Grade Level
     - Strand
     - Section
     - Enrollment Status
  5. You can edit a student by clicking the "Edit" button next to their name
  6. You can delete a student by clicking the "Delete" button (be careful!)

3.3 RE-ENROLL STUDENTS
-----------------------

Re-enrollment is used when moving students to a new school year. This usually 
happens at the start of a new school year.

To re-enroll students:
  1. Click "Student Management" in the left menu
  2. Click "Re-Enroll Students" from the submenu
  3. Select the previous school year from the dropdown
  4. You will see a list of students eligible for re-enrollment
  5. Select the students you want to re-enroll (check the boxes)
  6. Choose the new grade level (usually Grade 11 becomes Grade 12)
  7. Select the section for the students
  8. Click "Re-Enroll Selected" button
  9. The students will be enrolled in the current school year

NOTE: Grade 11 students automatically become Grade 12 when re-enrolled. Grade 12 
students who are re-enrolling must provide a reason.


4. STRAND AND SECTION MANAGEMENT
---------------------------------

This section allows you to manage the strands (like ABM, STEM, etc.) and 
sections (like Grade 11-A, Grade 11-B, etc.) in your school.

4.1 MANAGE STRANDS
------------------

To add or manage strands:
  1. Click "Strand and Section" in the left menu
  2. You will see a list of existing strands
  3. To add a new strand:
     - Click "Add Strand" button
     - Enter the strand name (e.g., "ABM", "STEM", "HUMSS")
     - Enter a description (optional)
     - Click "Save"
  4. To edit a strand:
     - Click "Edit" next to the strand name
     - Make your changes
     - Click "Save"
  5. To delete a strand:
     - Click "Delete" next to the strand name
     - Confirm the deletion

4.2 MANAGE SECTIONS
-------------------

To add or manage sections:
  1. Click "Strand and Section" in the left menu
  2. Go to the "Sections" tab
  3. You will see a list of existing sections
  4. To add a new section:
     - Click "Add Section" button
     - Enter the section name (e.g., "Grade 11-A")
     - Select the grade level (11 or 12)
     - Select the strand
     - Enter the maximum capacity (how many students can be in this section)
     - Click "Save"
  5. To edit a section:
     - Click "Edit" next to the section name
     - Make your changes
     - Click "Save"
  6. To delete a section:
     - Click "Delete" next to the section name
     - Confirm the deletion

IMPORTANT: You cannot delete a section if it has students assigned to it.


5. REPORTS
----------

Reports help you see information about students, teachers, and enrollment in 
different ways.

5.1 STUDENT LIST BY SECTION
----------------------------

This report shows all students organized by their sections.

To view:
  1. Click "Reports" in the left menu
  2. Click "Student List by Section" from the submenu
  3. Select a section from the dropdown
  4. You will see all students in that section
  5. You can export this list to a file if needed

5.2 TEACHER ASSIGNMENT REPORT
-----------------------------

This report shows which teachers are assigned to which subjects and sections.

To view:
  1. Click "Reports" in the left menu
  2. Click "Teacher Assignment Report" from the submenu
  3. You will see a list of all teachers with their assigned subjects and 
     sections
  4. You can export this report to a file

5.3 ENROLLMENT SUMMARY
----------------------

This report shows statistics about student enrollment.

To view:
  1. Click "Reports" in the left menu
  2. Click "Enrollment Summary" from the submenu
  3. You will see:
     - Total number of students
     - Number of students by grade level
     - Number of students by strand
     - Number of students by enrollment status
     - Number of students by section

5.4 STUDENT LOGS
----------------

This shows the history of all student records, including archived or deleted 
students.

To view:
  1. Click "Reports" in the left menu
  2. Click "STUDENT LOGS" from the submenu
  3. You will see all student records with their status
  4. You can restore archived students if needed


6. SETTINGS
-----------

Settings allow you to manage your account, teacher accounts, and school year 
information.

6.1 PROFILE
-----------

To view or edit your profile:
  1. Click "SETTINGS" in the left menu
  2. Click "User Accounts" from the submenu
  3. Click "Profile" from the submenu
  4. You will see your profile information:
     - Username
     - Full Name
     - Email
     - Role
  5. You can update your information and click "Save"
  6. You can change your password:
     - Enter your current password
     - Enter your new password
     - Confirm your new password
     - Click "Change Password"

6.2 TEACHER ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------

This allows you to create, edit, and manage teacher accounts.

To manage teacher accounts:
  1. Click "SETTINGS" in the left menu
  2. Click "User Accounts" from the submenu
  3. Click "Teacher Accounts" from the submenu
  4. You will see a list of all teachers

To add a new teacher:
  1. Click "Add Teacher" button
  2. Fill in the form:
     - Username (required, must be unique)
     - Password (required)
     - Full Name (required)
     - Email (optional)
     - Active Status (check if teacher should be able to login)
  3. Click "Save"

To edit a teacher:
  1. Click "Edit" next to the teacher's name
  2. Make your changes
  3. Click "Save"

To assign subjects and sections to a teacher:
  1. Click "Assign Subjects/Sections" next to the teacher's name
  2. Select the subjects you want to assign (maximum 8 subjects per teacher)
  3. Select the sections for each subject
  4. Click "Save Assignment"

To activate or deactivate a teacher:
  1. Click "Activate" or "Deactivate" next to the teacher's name
  2. Deactivated teachers cannot log in to the system

6.3 SCHOOL YEAR MANAGEMENT
--------------------------

This allows you to manage school years and transition to a new school year.

To view school years:
  1. Click "SETTINGS" in the left menu
  2. Click "School Year Management" from the submenu
  3. You will see a list of all school years

To add a new school year:
  1. Click "Add School Year" button
  2. Enter the school year (e.g., "2025-2026")
  3. Enter the start date
  4. Enter the end date
  5. Click "Save"
  6. The system will automatically create semesters for this school year

To set a school year as current:
  1. Find the school year you want to make current
  2. Click "Set as Current" button
  3. Confirm the action

To transition to a new school year:
  1. Click "Transition to New Year" button
  2. Select the new school year from the dropdown
  3. Choose which students to carry over:
     - Check "Enrolled Students" to carry over enrolled students
     - Check "Pending Students" to carry over pending students
  4. Review the preview showing how many students will be carried over
  5. Click "Confirm Transition"
  6. The system will:
     - Make the new school year current
     - Copy students to the new school year
     - Increase grade levels (Grade 11 becomes Grade 12)
     - Set enrollment status to "Pending" (students need to be re-enrolled)

IMPORTANT: Transitioning to a new school year is a major operation. Make sure 
you have backed up your data and understand what will happen.


7. LOGOUT
---------

To log out of the system:
  1. Click the "Logout" button at the bottom of the left menu
  2. You will be returned to the login screen

3. VIEWING YOUR SUBJECTS
-------------------------

This section shows all the subjects that have been assigned to you.

To view your subjects:
  1. Click "Subjects" in the left menu
  2. You will see a list of all your assigned subjects
  3. For each subject, you can see:
     - Subject name
     - Grade level
     - Sections assigned to you
     - Number of students in each section

You can click on a subject to see more details about the sections and students 
in that subject.


4. VIEWING YOUR STUDENTS
------------------------

This section shows all the students in your classes, organized by section.

To view your students:
  1. Click "Sections and Students" in the left menu
  2. You will see a list of all sections you are assigned to
  3. Click on a section to see all students in that section
  4. For each student, you can see:
     - Student ID
     - Full Name
     - Grade Level
     - Strand
     - Contact Information

You can search for specific students using the search box at the top.

You can export the student list to a file if needed for your records.


5. ACCOUNT SETTINGS
-------------------

This allows you to view and update your account information.

To access account settings:
  1. Click "Account Settings" in the left menu
  2. You will see your account information:
     - Username
     - Full Name
     - Email Address
     - Role

To update your information:
  1. Make changes to the fields you want to update
  2. Click "Save" button
  3. A success message will appear

To change your password:
  1. Enter your current password
  2. Enter your new password
  3. Confirm your new password
  4. Click "Change Password" button
  5. A success message will appear

IMPORTANT: Remember your new password! If you forget it, contact the 
administrator to reset it.

To update your profile picture:
  1. Click "Choose File" or "Browse" button
  2. Select an image file from your computer
  3. Click "Upload" button
  4. Your profile picture will be updated

NOTE: The profile picture should be a square image (same width and height) for 
best results. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF.


6. LOGOUT
---------

To log out of the system:
  1. Click the "Logout" button at the bottom of the left menu
  2. You will be returned to the login screen


================================================================================
                            TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

COMMON ISSUES AND SOLUTIONS:

1. Cannot log in
   - Check that your username and password are correct
   - Make sure Caps Lock is not on
   - Contact administrator if your account is deactivated

2. Cannot see students or subjects
   - Make sure you have been assigned subjects and sections by the administrator
   - Contact administrator if you need assignments

3. Cannot save changes
   - Make sure all required fields are filled
   - Check for error messages on the screen
   - Make sure you have permission to make the change

4. System is slow
   - Close other programs to free up computer resources
   - Check your internet connection
   - Contact IT support if problem persists

5. Forgot password
   - Contact the administrator to reset your password
   - Do not share your password with anyone

6. Need help
   - Contact the system administrator
   - Check this manual for step-by-step instructions


================================================================================
                            IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

SECURITY:
  - Never share your username and password with anyone
  - Always log out when you are done using the system
  - Change your password regularly
  - Use a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)

DATA ENTRY:
  - Always double-check information before saving
  - Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*)
  - Save your work frequently

BACKUP:
  - The system automatically saves your data
  - However, it is good practice to export important reports regularly

UPDATES:
  - The system may be updated from time to time
  - New features may be added
  - Check with administrator for new features
